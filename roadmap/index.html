Roadmap

Stage 0

Soft-Launch EOS Incubator DAC

We believe EOS will be the future of Blockchains in the coming years. Therefore we commit all our initial efforts towards building out this ecosystem.

We do this through creating a community of dedicated talent and companies that all share the same goal:
discover and build market-facing dApps and DACs that provide true value to humanity.

Stage 1

Tune and Improve Incubator DAC

Once we created the Incubator DAC we will focus our energy on the core-community and find the right partners to improve every important process until we get a decentralized high-performance incubation network.
