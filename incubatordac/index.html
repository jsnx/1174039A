Why Incubator?

Many ideas fail before they are even validated. On the one hand, because inexperienced founders cannot find the right networks and resources. On the other hand, because the number of projects that achieve visibility with a high marketing budget is increasing, although the idea supposedly has little potential. They are displacing better, but less well-financed projects. The buzz was bought with a marketing budget, while other promising projects lie fallow, as the necessary resources are lacking to carry out a reliable validation on the market.

 

Many (blockchain) projects and communities lack incentive mechanisms that go beyond traditional marketing measures. The solution lies in the technology itself: 

“Blockchains are incentive machines.”
Trent McConaghy

A big difference between the Blockchain ecosystem and traditional ecosystems is the community idea. Almost all popular blockchain projects are community-driven. We want to give projects the right incentive tools to activate a community. With the right incentives, faster validation is achieved and then market maturity is achieved more quickly.

“... the core feature of tokenized ecosystems, aka public blockchains, is getting people to do stuff.”
Trent McConaghy
